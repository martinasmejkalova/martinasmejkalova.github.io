About Me

Hello there!

My name is Martina, and I'm a girl from Prague (Czechia) who has a background in International Business and Finance. Currently, I am focused on expanding my skillset in the field of Data Analysis, specifically in Data and Business Intelligence.

I have always been fascinated by the way data can help organizations and businesses make better decisions. That's why I decided to dive deeper into this field and learn new technologies that enable me to extract valuable insights from large data sets.

I started to learn Tableau, where I create interactive visualizations that showcase complex data in a simple and intuitive manner. I also use Power BI for some of my projects and have been working with SQL to analyze data from various sources. I often retrieve data from platforms like Kaggle to perform exploratory data analysis, build predictive models and present insights in a more meaningful way.

On my GitHub page, you will find all my codes and relevant information about my projects. Below, you can find some of the final dashboards I have created that provide insights into various data sets.

Thank you for visiting my page, and please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or would like to collaborate on a project.
